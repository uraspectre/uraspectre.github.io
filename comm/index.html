no human characters allowed (if the character feels too close to human to me, i'll likely turn it down).
if the character has intricate details, i'll likely simplify them.
i prefer drawing softcore stuff. videogame-y stuff and bdsm are fun themes (i'm pretty squeamish, so there's some stuff there that's off-limits for me.)
explicit content is okay as long as you're fine with those parts of the body not being very detailed.

i won't draw: feral, hyper, gore, vore, inflation/weight gain, diaper/scat/urine/fart.

contact me on one of my social media accounts.
i highly encourage having a concept in mind already, preferably with reference images (even if those images are stick figures).
justsketch.me is also a good free pose doll.

payment is done via PayPal invoice.
sketch commissions must be paid for up front; other commissions require payment once a sketch is approved.
